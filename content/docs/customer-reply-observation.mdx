Customer Reply Observation is read-only outcome hydration after a sent Copilot SMS. It checks messaging thread replies after the outbound anchor message.

## Define Customer Reply Observation in WizField AI

WizField AI can observe whether a customer replied after a Copilot SMS was sent. The observation is derived from messaging thread records.

The observation starts from the outbound anchor message and looks for the first later inbound message in the same conversation.

## Keep Observation Read-Only

Customer Reply Observation does not mutate CRM records, send follow-up messages, retry messages, or start drip campaigns.

Observation should not become an action engine.

## Avoid Causal and Self-Learning Claims

Customer Reply Observation is temporal correlation in a messaging thread. It is not causal attribution, marketing ROI proof, or self-learning behavior.

WizField should not claim that a reply proves the AI caused the outcome.
