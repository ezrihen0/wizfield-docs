A Publish Job is the Growth Center record that controls outbound publishing. Draft metadata alone does not post content.

## Define Publish Job in Growth Center

Growth Center publishing is always explicit. A publish-now action or scheduled publishing action creates a Publish Job.

The Publish Job has its own UTC `scheduled_at` value for scheduled publishing or represents a publish-now action.

## Separate Publish Jobs From Draft Metadata

A content draft can include scheduling metadata, but draft `scheduled_at` is metadata only.

Growth Center must not silently post content from draft metadata, opportunity conversion, campaign placement, or automation runs.

## Track Publish Attempts

Publish Jobs can produce publish attempts. Attempt-level outcomes can record provider execution details.

Publish attempts are not reach, impression, click, ROI, or provider engagement metrics.
