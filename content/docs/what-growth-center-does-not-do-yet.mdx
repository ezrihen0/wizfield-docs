---
title: What Growth Center Does Not Do Yet
description: Understand the Growth Center V1 boundaries for Instagram publishing, monetization gates, AI copy generation, analytics, and auto-publish behavior.
---

Growth Center V1 does not include Instagram outbound publishing, runtime monetization gates, AI copy generation runtime, warehouse analytics rollups, provider engagement metrics, SEO engine behavior, or auto-publish maturity features.

## Do Not Expect Instagram Outbound Publishing in V1

Instagram outbound publishing is deferred from Growth Center V1. Instagram copy variants may exist as seeded studio tracks, but that is not the same as outbound Instagram publishing.

Growth Center docs and support responses should not describe Instagram posting as shipped V1 behavior.

## Do Not Expect Runtime Monetization Gates

Growth Center capabilities today are derived from role and product permissions in the Growth Center foundation. Runtime Growth Center monetization and Stripe entitlement gates are not wired into the marketing module.

Future monetization options remain architecture-only until explicitly implemented.

## Do Not Expect AI Copy Generation Runtime

Growth Center V1 does not include AI copy generation runtime inside Growth Center. Growth Center drafts, variants, opportunities, campaigns, automations, and publishing should not be described as AI-generated unless a future canonical source changes that.

## Do Not Treat Analytics as Provider Engagement Reporting

Growth Center analytics are internal funnel analytics. They are not ROI dashboards, ad dashboards, reach reports, impression reports, click reports, or provider engagement metrics.

Warehouse or nightly marketing analytics rollup infrastructure is also outside the shipped V1 scope.

## Do Not Expect Auto-Publish From Automations

Automations V1 can suggest or create drafts. Automations V1 does not auto-publish.

Selective auto-publish after review maturity, scheduled scanner triggers beyond opportunity-facing V1, exports, and deeper draft provenance remain deferred scope.
