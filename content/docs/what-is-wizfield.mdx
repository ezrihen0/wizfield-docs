WizField is a field-service operating system for owners who manage one or more service businesses from one account. It helps organize customer records, leads, jobs, estimates, invoices, communications, reports, and customer access.

## How WizField Supports Field-Service Operations

WizField is built around the daily operating needs of field-service businesses. The product direction is not a generic CRM reskin; WizField adapts a proven field-service CRM operating engine into a multi-tenant SaaS product.

WizField focuses on operational work such as:

- customer history
- lead intake
- job management
- estimates and invoices
- payments
- calls and SMS
- inspections and reports
- warranty documents
- dispatch and scheduling

## How WizField Separates Users and Businesses

WizField treats users and businesses as separate concepts. A user is a global identity, while an organization is the top-level tenant boundary for business data.

Memberships connect users to organizations. Roles and permissions belong to those memberships, not to the user globally. This model allows one user to access multiple businesses while keeping each business's records scoped to the active organization.

## How WizField Fits With Multi-Business Ownership

WizField supports owners who operate more than one service business. Multi-business capability is part of the product model, not an edge case.

When a user has access to multiple organizations, WizField uses an explicit organization switcher flow. After switching, WizField changes the active organization context for dashboard, customers, leads, jobs, estimates, invoices, settings, search, calls, messaging, reports, booking, and portal-adjacent context.
